ACCESSIBILITY

Crayon Designs is committed to making its web pages accessible to everyone.

The Worldwide Web Consortium has produced guidelines for making web content accessible to people with disabilities. We have designed our website based on these guidelines. For example, we have provided text alternatives for any non-text content and have made most functionaility available from a keyboard.
